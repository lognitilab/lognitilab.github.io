#### Anmol Pahwa

Anmol Pahwa is an Assistant Professor in Civil Engineering Department at Indian Institute of Technology - Madras, with a particular focus in Transportation Engineering. He holds a BTech in Civil Engineering from Indian Institute of Techology - Delhi, and a PhD in Civil and Environmental Engineering from University of California, Davis. Before joing IIT Madras, Anmol worked a postdoctoral scholar at Insititute of Transportation Studies, UC Davis. His research interests include sustainable transportation, freight transportation, city logistics, last-mile, and operations research.
